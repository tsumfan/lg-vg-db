Scrolling Shooter

Each Cotton game features a unique story that is loosely connected to the other games in the series. All of these stories revolve around the obsession of a young witch named Nata de Cotton with a magical type of candy called “Willow”. Cotton is a red-haired pre-teen witch with a hunger for Willow candy that drives all of her actions. She is hot-headed, immature, and often overreacts. Source: Wikipedia
